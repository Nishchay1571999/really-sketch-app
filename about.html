Who made this?

Just a guy, on his computer at 2:00 am, trying to make cool stuff. I grew up with a desire to design and build things, and access to graph paper from my dad, which I used all the time.

I wanted to capture that ease of quickly sketching an idea on a sheet of graph paper, in a digital way, without having to learn a complicated piece of software. Disappointed with the options available online, and insired by some people who had built their own, I decided to build what I wanted.
